I specialise in teaching English speaking, grammar, reading, writing and listening skills to corporate clients in London.

My clients range from technology firms keen to improve the English language skills of their software developers, to business professionals who wish to improve their conversational English.

A detailed needs analysis is performed before, at the beginning and throughout your course to ensure that you get exactly what you want from your lessons. I also provide a monthly report to highlight areas of improvement and those areas that need extra attention, and give you advice on how to improve between our meetings, which includes making digital lesson and homework notes available to you online.

I can help you with...

  • ✔   Achieving the First Certificate (FCE), Advanced Certificate (CAE), Proficiency Certificate (CPE) & IELTS Cambridge English qualifications
  • ✔   Improving your business English - this can be focused on a particular profession, such as technology or banking.
  • ✔   Building personal relationships
  • ✔   Communicating more effectively when travelling
  • ✔   Accent reduction
