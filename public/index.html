About me

I specialise in teaching English speaking, grammar, reading, writing and listening skills to corporate and individual clients in London.

My clients range from technology firms keen to improve the English language skills of their software developers, to business professionals who wish to improve their conversational English.

Whether your goal is to communicate more effectively in work situations or to pass formal exams such as the First Certificate (FCE), Advanced Certificate (CAE), Proficiency Certificate (CPE) & IELTS Cambridge English qualifications, I can help you get where you want to be.

1-to-1 training

1-to-1 training sessions allow for rapid individual development of a client's ability to communicate in spoken and written English.

  • Fun, engaging lessons, tailored to your needs
  • We can focus your business English on a particular profession, such as technology or banking
  • Regular progress reports
